Chapter Roadmap

CHAPTER 1

Your First Three.js Scene

Create a rotating cube, learn the renderer, scene, camera, and understand the animation loop.

CHAPTER 2

Transformations & Hierarchy

Master position, rotation, scale, Euler angles, quaternions, and scene graphs with groups.

CHAPTER 3

Cameras & Camera Control

Explore perspective and orthographic cameras, then learn OrbitControls and PointerLock.

CHAPTER 4

Geometry, Meshes & Materials

Create cubes, spheres, planes, cylinders, and toruses with realistic materials.

CHAPTER 5

Lights, Shadows & Realism

Add ambient, directional, point, and spot lights, then enable realistic shadow mapping.

CHAPTER 6

Animation, Timing & Input

Master delta time, keyboard controls, mouse input, and frame-rate independent movement.

CHAPTER 7

Raycasting & Object Interaction

Pick objects with raycasts, handle selection, detect collisions, and build intuitive interactions.

CHAPTER 8

Textures, UV Mapping & Advanced Materials

Load textures, apply UV mapping, use normal maps, roughness, metalness, and environment maps.

CHAPTER 9

Skyboxes, Fog & Particles

Create immersive environments with cube textures, atmospheric fog, and sprite-based particles.

CHAPTER 10

Loading Models & Animation

Import GLTF/Blender models, use AnimationMixer for skeletal animation, and load assets.

CHAPTER 11

Post-Processing & Custom Shaders

Apply bloom, SSAO, tone mapping, write custom GLSL shaders, and create visual effects.

CHAPTER 12

Performance & Advanced Topics

Optimize rendering, manage GPU memory, use InstancedMesh, debug, and follow best practices.
